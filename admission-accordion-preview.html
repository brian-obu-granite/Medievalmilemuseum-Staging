Getting Here
By Car

Search for Medieval Mile Museum, Kilkenny in your preferred navigation app. Public parking is available within walking distance of the museum, with several nearby car parks located throughout Kilkenny city centre. We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes before your booked visit to allow time to park and make your way to the museum entrance.

By Bus

Regular bus services connect Kilkenny with Dublin, Waterford, Cork and other major towns, with the main bus stops just a short walk from the museum.

On foot

The Medieval Mile Museum is centrally located on Kilkenny’s Medieval Mile and is easily reached on foot from anywhere in the city centre.

*We would advise that visitors arrive at least 15 minutes ahead of time for the Walking Trail.
